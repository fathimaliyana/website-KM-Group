About

KM Group, established in 1994, aims to support the people of northern Kerala, particularly in Palakkad, Malapuram, and Calicut Districts by providing live broiler chicken, fresh chicken, poultry feed, and day-old chicks. The firm is located in Alanallur Village, Palakkad District, Kerala is run by Chairman Mr.Muhammadhali K and his 2 sons Mr.Muhammed Fayis K and Mr.Firoz K.

Our unique combination of innovative customer service and product quality with competitive pricing made us to improve our self as a better one.
